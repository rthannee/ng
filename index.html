Welcome to Namitha Group
Namitha Group, established in 2007,  is a conglomeration of Namitha Builders - a real estate enterprise, Nekstgen Cybernetics – a software enterprise currently under Internet of Things space and Namitha Foundation - a CSR initiative of the group, driven by the the philosophies of Sri Adishankara Charya.

The Moto of Namitha is to bring in Serenity to mankind whilst inhabiting in a practical and affordable luxurious lifestyle.
The Three Pillars

INVOLVE

We fathom with deep sense of pursuit on how to transcend the lifestyle of an individual thereby transforming the society.

RESOLVE

We Redefine industry's best practices and abreast latest technologies to break the economies of scales for providing a practical and affordable lifestyle.

EVOLVE

We bring in an ecosystem which helps in uplifting under privileged and empowering the privileged and usher them to tranquility.

Verticals

NAMITHA BUILDERS

NAMITHA builders is one of the fastest growing real estate enterprises in Hyderabad. Established in 2007 by Late Sri Ch. Srinivas, the company has crafted scores of lifestyle apartments, malls and multiplexes in premium locales of the city. The pioneering efforts of the company are strengthened by a skilled team of engineers, architects, structural engineers, landscape designers and consultants in marketing and finance.

NEKSTGEN

Nekstgen Cybernetics is a software company, currently into Internet of Things domain, which develops, designs, manufactures and markets Smart home products. Technically, we give you intelligent homes and smarter living by creating software, fabricating hardware and combing through terabytes of data.

NAMITHA FOUNDATION

Namitha Foundation, a corporate social responsibility wing of Namitha group, aims at contributing to the well being of people by detoxifying the body, mind and soul through the principles of Sri Adisankaracharya. Namitha Foundation in collaboration with Sri Sri Sri Adisankaracharya Bhakti Samajam believes in the principles of Advaita doctrine by Sri Jagadguru and aims at propagating the essence of Jagadguru's philosophy there by bringing stability and Serenity to each individual.
